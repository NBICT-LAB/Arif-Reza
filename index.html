About

I am a technically sound, innovative, self-motivated, and multifaceted Environmental Scientist with 11+ years of experience in addressing issues related to environmental sustainability, water quality, wastewater treatment, and nutrient stewardship. I am also skilled and experienced in innovating and modifying need-based concepts, approaches and theories, conducting initial research, data management, scientific writing, grant writing, participating in research projects with cross-functional research teams, designing outcome-based course curricula, and student assessment. In general, I would like to utilize my interdisciplinary academic knowledge and practical experience to ensure environmental and societal well-being.
